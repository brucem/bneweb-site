About the Brisbane Web Design Group

Want to rewrite this!

The Brisbane Web Design Group is a local, non-profit and self-organised monthly event aimed at bringing together the creative people around Brisbane to connect, discuss and learn about all things web and design related.

Doors are open to everyone.

Come along and meet fellow website designers, web developers and web business people near you!

We include people from various disciplines....

  • Graphic Design
  • Web Design
  • Web Development
  • Internet Marketing (SEO and PPC)
  • Online Business and much more

...so there are always lots of interesting discussions happening and an opportunity to make valuable contacts as you mingle with new friends!

If you would like get involved to present, volunteer, provide support or become a sponsor please get in touch with us.

Event Location

Brisbane Web Design events are held at:

The Edge
State Library of Queensland,
Stanley Place, South Bank,
Brisbane, Australia

Times and schedule

Each event runs to a similar schedule. Doors open at 5:30pm with the formal presentations starting at around 6:00pm. Depending on the speakers the presentations will finish at or before 8:00pm.

After the presentations we convene at a local pub to catch-up in a less formal setting. It's a great opportunity to meet other local web industry participants and discuss the evenings presentations.
